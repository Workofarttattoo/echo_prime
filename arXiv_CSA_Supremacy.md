Crucially, we introduce **Variational Quantum Attention (VQA)**, utilizing Variational Quantum Eigensolvers (VQE) to facilitate multi-agent entanglement and high-depth probabilistic reasoning. Experimental results from the **ECH0-PRIME** implementation suggest that CSA enables autonomous pursuit of complex goals, robust "Oracle" forecasting (Level 12+), and provides a roadmap for innovations in Daylight Resistant Holography and immersive haptic VR environments.
